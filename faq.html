Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about BraapTrax before you hit the throttle.

The App
BraapTrax is a ride tracking app for iPhone built specifically for dirt bikers. Think of your favorite fitness tracker — but built for off-road riding. It gives you GPS route tracking, post-ride stats, bike garage management, service logging, and crew social features all in one place.
Yes — BraapTrax is live on the App Store. Download it here and start tracking your rides today.
BraapTrax requires iOS 16 or later. It's optimized for iPhone but an iPad version may come later. Android support is not planned at this time.
Yes — BraapTrax is free to download from the App Store. Core features are free, with optional premium features planned for the future.
Ride Tracking
Tap "Start Ride" and BraapTrax uses your iPhone's GPS to record your route in real time. It tracks your path on a map, measures distance, speed, and elevation. When you're done, tap "End Ride" and your full stats card is ready immediately.
Auto-pause detects when you've stopped moving and pauses the ride timer automatically. When you start moving again, it resumes. This keeps your stats clean — you won't have long idle stretches padding your time or distance numbers.
Yes — GPX export is planned so you can share your route files with friends, upload to other platforms, or keep your own backups. This feature will be available at or shortly after launch.
Garage & Maintenance
You can add multiple bikes to your garage. Each bike tracks its own rides and maintenance history separately, so you always know which machine has what on it.
Log service events like oil changes, air filter swaps, or any other work you've done. Set service intervals and BraapTrax will surface upcoming maintenance so nothing slips through the cracks before a big ride.
Social Features
After a ride you can tag the friends you rode with. Tagged riders get a notification about the ride but don't get a duplicate in their own history — it's purely a social tag. Over time, BraapTrax surfaces your top 5 riding partners based on how often you ride together.
Yes — season-long leaderboards and head-to-head friend comparisons are planned for the stats section. See who in your crew has the most miles, highest top speed, or most rides this season.
